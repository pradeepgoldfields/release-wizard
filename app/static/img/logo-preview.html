Conduit — Logo Concepts

Theme: everything passes through it

01
Many-to-Many Flow
Streams converge into a central node then fan back out. Conduit as universal junction.
02
The Pipe
Literal dark tube with particles flowing left→right. Entry and exit arrows. Most direct.
03
The Gateway
Hexagonal portal with a single flow line piercing through its centre. Conduit as checkpoint.
04
Infinity Loop
Figure-8 crossover — releases continuously cycle through. Continuous delivery metaphor.
05
Tunnel Cross-Section
Looking end-on into the pipe — concentric rings drawing you in. Depth and perspective.
06
Many-into-One
Three coloured streams braid and merge into a single unified output. Clean funnel metaphor.
07
Pipe End / C-Mark
The letter C as a hollow pipe cross-section with flow exiting. Clever typographic mark.
08
Shield + Beam
A security shield with a glowing beam cutting straight through. Safe passage — nothing blocked.
09
Signal / Waveform
A signal pulse on a flat line — Conduit transmits events and triggers across the platform.

All on white
